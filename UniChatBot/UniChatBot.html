What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a software application designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the internet. Chatbots can be programmed to understand and respond to text or spoken language, enabling them to interact with users in a way that mimics human conversation.

Why student must use UniChatBot?

UniChatBot is available 24/7, allowing students to access information whenever they need it, regardless of time zones or office hours.UniChatbots can collect data on student interactions, helping universities identify common challenges, track student progress, and make data-driven decisions to improve educational services.
